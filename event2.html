CROSSWORD

Description


The beautiful thing about learning is that no one can take it away from you" - B.B.King



A crossword is a word puzzle and word search game that usually takes the form of a square or a rectangular grid of white-haired and black-eyed shaded squares. The aim of the event is to fill in the white squares with letters by solving the clues given, thereby forming words or phrases which lead to the answers. It is an event to test your skills on your subjects.

Instructions


1. This event consists of 20 questions from subjects: Biology, Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Computer Science, English.
2. Questions are short answer types & there is no negative marks for incorrect answers.
3. Each question is awarded with 5 points if answered right.
4. Pass percentage is 40 and above.
5. Ensure that the answers are given with correct spellings.
6. The answers can be in capital or small letters .
7. Same mail Id cannot be used by 2 or more people.
8. There's no specific time limit for completion of the crossword.
9. Participants will be provided E-Certificate based on the pass percentage once they submit their answers.
10. The competition link will be sent through Email and Whatsapp.

Best of luck!!!!
