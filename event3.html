ONLINE QUIZ

Description


Tired of quarantine blues? Wanna brush up your academic content through fun trivia game quizzes? We've got your back! Register, to spruce up and participate in this online live trivia quiz.

When?

Each day from 29th june to 4th july.
Time: 4:30 PM and 6:30 PM each day (2 sessions each day)

How?

The link to participate in the live streaming of the quiz will be displayed in google meet.The link for google meet will be shared in the event whatsApp group every morning. The link will be different for all the days . So, participants who wish to take part in any particular days' quiz must check our website Or the whatsApp group for the updated link.

All the participants will be provided with an e-certificate and 3 top winners will be provided a winner certificate shortly after you submit the feedback form.


Instructions


1. Once you enter the google meeting, a demo video will be shared and the registration link will be posted . Fill the registration form and submit it.
2. ( COMPULSARY ) Once you click on the live streaming link, you will be asked to enter a screen name . You must enter it in this format : Name-contact number
3. Then wait for a few minutes until the presenter starts the quiz as the presenter waits for more participants to join.
4. Entries after the mentioned session are not valid. So kindly be punctual. The quiz is live so participants who enter late won't be able to participate fully.
5. Questions will be picked from physics, chemistry, biology, computer science and aptitude. All are MULTIPLE CHOICE QUESTIONS ONLY.
6. At the end of the quiz your position will be displayed ; KINDLY TAKE A SCREENSHOT of it immediately for further references.
7. It would be feasible if you install google meet on your device prior to the event.
8. Participants can take part in more than one session. There is no limit.
9. Participants are RECOMMENDED TO USE COMPUTER OR LAPTOP for much convenience and ease. Mobile phones are also allowed.
10. The link to the feedback form will be displayed at the end of the quiz on live, so participants can copy the link and paste it in the url tab as shown in the quiz page and submit their feedback to receive certificates.



IT'S AN ONLINE LIVE TRIVIA QUIZ. THE LINK IS INVALID BEFORE AND AFTER THE SCHEDULED SHIFT TIME.KINDLY BE PRESENT AT THE MENTIONED TIME.
