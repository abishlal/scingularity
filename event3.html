Description


Tired of quarantine blues? Wanna brush up your academic content through fun trivia game quizzes? We've got your back! Register, to spruce up and participate in this online live trivia quiz.

When?

Each day from 29th june to 4th july.
Time: 5 PM and 7 PM each day

How?

The link to participate in the live streaming of the quiz will be displayed on our website and it will also be shared in the event whatsApp group/ email. The link will be different for all the days . So, participants who wish to take part in any particular days' quiz must check our website Or the whatsApp group for the updated link.

All the participants will be provided with an e-certificate and the winners will be provided a winner certificate.


Instructions


1. Once you click on the live streaming link, you will be asked to enter a screen name . You must enter it in this format : name-contact number.
2. Then wait for a few minutes until the presenter starts the quiz as the presenter waits for more participants to join.
3. Entries after the mentioned shift time are not valid. So kindly be punctual.
4. Once the quiz starts, questions will be displayed one by one for which you must select the correct answer.
5. All the participants will be awarded with e-certificates
6. PRO TIP : Answer faster to get bonus points.
7. At the end of the quiz your position will be displayed ; kindly take a screenshot of it
